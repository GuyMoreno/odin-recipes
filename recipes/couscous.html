Couscous

Description

Couscous is a versatile North African dish made from steamed durum wheat granules. Light and fluffy, it pairs beautifully with vegetables, meats, and rich sauces.

Ingredients

  • Couscous (durum wheat or whole grain)
  • Vegetables (carrots, zucchini, bell peppers, tomatoes)
  • Chickpeas or other legumes
  • Onions or shallots
  • Garlic
  • Spinach or kale
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro, mint)

Steps

  1. Chop Ingredients: Dice vegetables and rinse chickpeas.
  2. Sauté: Heat olive oil in a skillet, add onions and garlic, and cook until soft.
  3. Sauté:Cook Veggies: Add chopped vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Sauté:Add Chickpeas: Stir in chickpeas and spices, cooking for a few more minutes.
  5. Prepare Couscous:In a pot, bring vegetable broth to a boil, then stir in couscous. Remove from heat and cover for 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff Couscous: Use a fork to fluff the couscous.
  7. Combine: Mix the veggies and chickpeas into the couscous. Add lemon juice and adjust seasoning.
  8. Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs and enjoy!
