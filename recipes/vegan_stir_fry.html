Vegan Stir-Fry

Description

This vibrant vegan stir-fry features tender udon noodles tossed with sautéed garlic, ginger, bell pepper, and carrot, all topped with golden tofu and creamy coconut sauce. Quick, nutritious, and full of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Small piece ginger, minced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, sliced
  • 100g udon noodles
  • 100g firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/3 cup coconut cream
  • Bean sprouts, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook Noodles: Boil until soft, then drain.
  2. Fry Tofu: Heat oil, fry tofu until golden. Set aside.
  3. Stir-Fry:Sauté garlic and ginger. Add bell pepper and carrot.
  4. combine:Add soy sauce, tofu, noodles, and coconut cream. Heat through.
  5. Serve:Top with bean sprouts. Enjoy!
